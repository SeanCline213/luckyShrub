Welcome to Lucky Shrub!

Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance and landscaping. The company also runs a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants, making them a one-stop shop for clients to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of".

Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.

Our Services

  • Landscape Design

    Transform your outdoor space into something truly special. Our landscape design services blend creativity with functionality to create custom layouts that reflect your style, enhance curb appeal, and thrive in your environment. From concept to completion, we design outdoor spaces that are beautiful, balanced, and built to last.

  • Landscape Maintenance

    Keep your landscape looking its best all year long. Our professional maintenance services ensure your lawn, plants, and outdoor features stay healthy, vibrant, and well‑kept. With reliable scheduling and attention to detail, we take the work out of upkeep so you can simply enjoy your outdoor space.

  • Tree Care

    Healthy trees make a powerful impact. Our expert tree care services promote safety, longevity, and natural beauty through proper pruning, trimming, and removal when necessary. Whether maintaining mature trees or caring for new growth, we help protect your property while preserving the landscape you love.
